About Me

Hi! I'm a junior at Saratoga High School with interests in Computer Science and Percussion. I love building things and hope to one day make the world a better place with software.

My Interests

Machine Learning

Until recently, I've perceived machine learning as magic. After learning how neural networks really work, I've made it a goal to use this technology to solve the world's pressing issues.

App Development

I began my journey into CS with a basic iOS app. I now use app development as a creative outlet for my ideas and machine learning models.

Percussion

Ever since I was young, I've loved to create rhythm and sound. My parents always wondered about me as I tapped away on the table, beatboxing and humming. It's no surprise I fell in love with this field of music.

Machine Learning

I've created ML models with both PyTorch and TensorFlow and implemented these models into a number of projects. I have experience using various neural network types and architectures for things like image classification and sentiment detection. These technologies are ones I frequently use in software development competitions, typically hackathons. If you're looking to get started with ML, here is a video I created that explains the basics of neural networks.


App Development

I use Swift and SwiftUI to create iOS apps. Having knowledge of mobile app development allows me to utilize mobile-specific techonologies, which is vital to projects like Find My Pet. In the future, I hope to learn to use React so I can aid more people with my future projects.


Percussion

I'm currently a member of the snareline at the Blue Devils 'B' Drum and Bugle Corps. Outside of drum corps, I participate in marching, winter, and concert percussion at Saratoga High School.
